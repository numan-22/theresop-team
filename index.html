THE RESOP TEAM

After traveling the world for his 14-year professional baseball career, Chris and Kara Resop returned to their hometown of Naples, Florida, to raise their two children. The small town feel with big city amenities, plus perfect year-round weather, attracted them just like it does the clients they serve.

Their core values of passionate expertise, discretion and exceptional client service have helped them successfully close over 200 transactions and a quarter-billion dollars’ worth of real estate since 2015. The Resop Team consistently ranks among the Top 1% of realtors nationally. With over 20 years of combined real estate experience, Chris and Kara offer clients an unrivaled commitment to excellence and insight into Southwest Florida’s luxury real estate market, helping them unite extraordinary lives with Southwest Florida’s finest properties.
